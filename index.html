What we do

STEM Junction is an annual bioinformatics symposium for advanced high school students. Bioinformatics is a new field that uses computer science and math to revolutionize the way biologists do research. STEM Junction is packed with talks on the newest discoveries and applications in sub-fields such as machine learning, computational modeling, gene discovery, electronic health records, data science ethics, and many more! STEM Junction Symposium is designed for high school teachers and their students and is the field trip that will change lives and influence career decisions. No prior experience in computer science or biology is necessary!
